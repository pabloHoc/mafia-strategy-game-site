# Game Overview

## High Concept

A dark urban fantasy strategy game where players build hidden organizations through leverage, assets, and internal politics.

Power is not what you own, but what you can leverage.

## Player Fantasy

Lead a clandestine organization competing for power in a living city.

Recruit lieutenants, acquire valuable assets, cultivate relationships, and pursue ambitions while navigating both external rivals and internal politics.

Grow from a small operation into a shadow power capable of reshaping the city.

## Core Gameplay Loop

1. Gather information and opportunities.
2. Acquire or develop assets.
3. Assign people and resources to missions.
4. Expand the organization's influence.
5. Manage ambitions, loyalty, and internal politics.
6. Pursue long-term goals while adapting to an evolving world.

## Core Concepts

### Assets

Assets represent leverage over the world.

Examples include:

- Organizations
- Businesses
- Secrets
- Favors
- Relics
- Agents

### Organizations

Organizations are structures of people, assets, and influence.

Growth comes from delegation, hierarchy, and trust.

### Missions

Missions commit assets to pursue opportunities under uncertainty.

Every commitment carries risk and opportunity cost.

### Districts

Districts are opportunity spaces rather than owned territory.

They evolve over time and provide unique challenges and rewards.

### Information

Information is valuable and imperfect.

Players rarely possess complete knowledge of rivals or the world.

## Progression

Power grows through organizational development rather than territorial conquest.

Early game focuses on survival and expansion.

Mid game focuses on specialization and delegation.

Late game focuses on managing complexity, ambition, and succession.

## Endgame

The goal is not merely to accumulate wealth or territory, but to build an enduring organization capable of shaping the hidden world.

The greatest threat to a powerful organization may come from within.
